Web Workshop #005 - So You Want a Website

About

Anyone can make a website. What’s difficult is creating a website that captures your target audience, delivers the right information and creates an enjoyable experience.

There are millions of websites out there that are flawed because the people involved didn’t ask the right questions at the start. Whether you’re a designer, developer, business owner or just someone who wants a website, there’s a bunch of questions you need to answer before you even think about buying URLS, designing pages or even asking someone else to make you a website.

This session is all about the key questions you need to ask and answer at the start of any website project. You’ll need to bring along an idea for a website, could be real, could be fictitious. Then during the session we’ll go about answering these questions like ‘Why the hell do you even want a website?’ and ‘Who the hell is going to use it?’. The answers to these will form the foundation of your website project and help you create a site that is focused, relevant and well structured. The process you’ll learn can be applied to any web project so you can use it time and again to help you get off on the right foot.
