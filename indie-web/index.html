Web Workshop #001 - The IndieWeb

About

The IndieWeb is about taking ownership of your own content. In this workshop we will cover the fundamental ideas surrounding the IndieWeb and how to implement some of them. You’ll have the chance to do something most web developers rarely do – work on your own website!

When you post something on the web, it should belong to you, not a corporation. Too many companies have gone out of business and lost all of their users’ data. By joining the IndieWeb, your content stays yours and in your control.

What you need

  • A laptop with a coding environment
  • A drink (non alcoholic)
  • Knowledge of basic web development techniques
  • Your own domain and hosting
  • A ticket from Eventbrite
